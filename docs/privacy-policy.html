Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 9, 2025

Welcome to Content AI ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Introduction

Content AI is an AI-powered content generation platform that creates tweets, Instagram posts, blog articles, and images using artificial intelligence. This Privacy Policy details how we collect, use, and protect your information.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Account Information

  • Authentication Data: When you sign in with Google or Apple, we collect your email address and basic profile information
  • User Profile: Name, email address, and profile picture from your chosen authentication provider
  • Device Information: Device ID for welcome credit tracking and fraud prevention

2.2 Usage Data

  • Content Requests: The prompts and requests you submit for content generation
  • Generated Content: AI-generated text and images created for you (stored temporarily)
  • Credit Usage: Records of credit consumption and subscription status
  • App Analytics: Usage patterns, feature usage, and performance metrics

2.3 Technical Data

  • Device Data: Device type, operating system, app version
  • Network Data: IP address, network connection type
  • Error Logs: Crash reports and error logs for debugging

3. Detailed Data Collection (Apple App Store Requirements)

In accordance with Apple's App Store privacy requirements, we collect the following data categories:

3.1 Contact Info

  • Email Address: Required for account creation and authentication
  • Name: From your Google/Apple account for personalization
  • Purpose: User authentication, account management, and communication
  • Linked to Identity: Yes

3.2 User Content

  • Photos: Images you generate and save to your device
  • Other User Content: Text prompts, generated tweets, blog posts, and other content
  • Purpose: Content generation, service provision, and temporary storage
  • Linked to Identity: Yes
  • Retention: Generated content deleted after 30 days

3.3 Identifiers

  • User ID: Unique identifier from authentication provider
  • Device ID: iOS identifierForVendor for welcome credit tracking
  • Purpose: Account management, fraud prevention, and personalization
  • Linked to Identity: Yes

3.4 Usage Data

  • Product Interaction: Features used, buttons clicked, time spent in app
  • App Performance: Crash logs, response times, error rates
  • Purpose: Service improvement, analytics, and debugging
  • Linked to Identity: Yes

3.5 Diagnostics

  • Crash Data: App crashes and exceptions
  • Performance Data: App launch times, memory usage
  • Purpose: Bug fixes, performance optimization
  • Linked to Identity: No

4. Third-Party AI Services

  • OpenAI: Your content prompts are sent to OpenAI for content generation
  • DALL-E 3: Image generation requests processed by OpenAI
  • Web Search: Real-time web data integrated for enhanced content
  • Data Sharing: Prompts shared with AI providers for processing only
  • Retention: We do not train AI models with your data

5. How We Use Your Information

  • Service Provision: Generate AI content based on your prompts
  • Account Management: Authenticate users and manage subscriptions
  • Credit System: Track usage and prevent abuse
  • Content Moderation: Filter inappropriate content for safety
  • Analytics: Improve app performance and user experience
  • Support: Provide customer service and technical support

6. Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with:

  • AI Providers: OpenAI for content generation (prompts only)
  • Authentication: Google/Apple for sign-in verification
  • Payment Processing: RevenueCat for subscription management
  • Cloud Services: Supabase for secure data storage
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect rights

7. Data Security

  • Encryption: All data transmitted using HTTPS/TLS encryption
  • Authentication: Secure OAuth 2.0 with Google/Apple
  • Access Control: Limited access to personal data
  • Data Retention: Generated content deleted after 30 days
  • Incident Response: Prompt notification of security breaches

8. Your Rights

  • Access: Request copies of your personal data
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Delete your account and associated data
  • Portability: Export your data in a structured format
  • Objection: Opt-out of non-essential data processing

9. Data Retention

  • Account Data: Retained while account is active
  • Generated Content: Automatically deleted after 30 days
  • Usage Analytics: Anonymized after 12 months
  • Support Data: Deleted after issue resolution
  • Legal Holds: Retained as required by law

10. Children's Privacy

Content AI is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately.

11. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for international transfers.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as our service evolves. Significant changes will be communicated through the app. Your continued use constitutes acceptance of updated terms.
