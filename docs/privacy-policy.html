Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 9, 2025

Welcome to Content AI ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application for AI-powered content generation.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Account Information

  • Google Sign-In: Email address, name, and profile photo from your Google account
  • Apple Sign-In: Email address and name from your Apple ID
  • Device Information: Unique device identifier to prevent credit abuse

1.2 Usage Data

  • Generated Content: AI-generated tweets, Instagram posts, blog articles, and YouTube scripts
  • Prompts and Inputs: Text you provide to generate content
  • Credit Usage: Record of credits consumed for different content types
  • Web Search Data: Search queries when using web-enhanced content generation

1.3 Technical Information

  • App Analytics: Feature usage, crash reports, and performance metrics
  • Device Details: iOS/Android version, app version, device model
  • Network Information: IP address for security and fraud prevention

2. How We Use Your Information

  • AI Content Generation: Process your prompts to create personalized content
  • Credit System: Track usage and prevent abuse with device-based limits
  • Web Search Enhancement: Improve content quality with real-time information
  • Subscription Management: Process RevenueCat payments and manage premium features
  • Content Moderation: Filter inappropriate content and ensure safety
  • Service Improvement: Analyze usage patterns to enhance AI models
  • Customer Support: Provide technical assistance and resolve issues

3. AI and Content Processing

  • OpenAI Integration: Your prompts are processed by OpenAI's GPT models
  • DALL-E 3: Image generation requests are sent to OpenAI's image API
  • Content Storage: Generated content is temporarily stored for delivery
  • No Training Data: Your content is not used to train our AI models

4. Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with:

  • OpenAI: For AI content generation (subject to OpenAI's privacy policy)
  • RevenueCat: For subscription and payment processing
  • Supabase: For secure data storage and user authentication
  • Google/Apple: For authentication services
  • Legal Authorities: When required by law or to protect our rights

5. Data Security

  • Encryption: All data is encrypted in transit and at rest
  • Authentication: Secure OAuth 2.0 with Google and Apple
  • Access Control: Strict access controls and monitoring
  • Regular Audits: Security reviews and vulnerability assessments

6. Your Rights

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Delete your account and associated data
  • Portability: Export your generated content
  • Objection: Opt-out of certain data processing

7. International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed in countries outside your residence, including the United States, where our AI services are hosted. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place.

8. Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected such information, please contact us immediately.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will notify you of significant changes through the app or by email.
