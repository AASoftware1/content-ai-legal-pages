🚀 Common Issues

Account & Authentication

  • Can't sign in: Try clearing app data and sign in again
  • Google Sign-In issues: Check your Google account permissions
  • Apple Sign-In issues: Verify your Apple ID is active

Credits & Billing

  • Missing credits: Welcome credits are granted once per device
  • Subscription issues: Manage subscriptions through your device settings
  • Billing questions: Contact us with your purchase details

Content Generation

  • Content not generating: Check your internet connection and credits
  • Poor quality results: Try more specific prompts
  • Inappropriate content blocked: Our moderation system filters unsafe content

🔧 Troubleshooting

  1. Restart the app: Close completely and reopen
  2. Check internet connection: AI generation requires stable internet
  3. Update the app: Ensure you have the latest version
  4. Clear cache: Try signing out and back in
